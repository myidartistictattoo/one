Remembrance Butterfly Tattoos: Symbolism And Meaning

Written by Feb 22, 2023 · 1 min read

Butterflies are often associated with beauty, grace, and elegance. They are also a popular symbol of freedom and transformation, making them a popular choice for tattoos. Butterfly tattoos can be small and subtle, or larger and more intricate. One type of butterfly tattoo that is becoming increasingly popular is the remembrance butterfly tattoo. This type of tattoo is meant to honor and remember someone who has passed away. It often includes a quote, date, or other meaningful symbol to represent the loved one.
