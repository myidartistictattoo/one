What Does It Mean To Have A Black Judge With Tattoos?

Written by Feb 27, 2023 · 1 min read

In the twenty-first century, tattoos have become increasingly popular amongst all demographics. However, it is still uncommon to see a black judge with tattoos. This is likely due to the fact that tattoos are often seen as rebellious and counter-culture. This view can be particularly damaging for minorities who are seen as part of those counter-culture movements. In a world where black people are often judged by the color of their skin, it is important to recognize that having a black judge with tattoos is a symbol of progress.
