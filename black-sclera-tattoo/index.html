What Is Black Sclera Tattoo?

Written by Mar 07, 2023 · 1 min read

A black sclera tattoo is a type of body modification that involves having a dark, inky color applied to the white part of the eye. It is a relatively new and rare form of body art, and it is becoming increasingly popular among individuals who are looking to express themselves in a unique and creative way. The process of having a black sclera tattoo applied involves having a special type of ink injected into the sclera, which is the white part of the eye. The ink is then absorbed into the sclera, creating a dark, inky color. The result is a dramatic, black-eyed look that can be quite striking.
