A Guide To Female Inner Thigh Tattoos In 2023

Written by Mar 10, 2023 · 1 min read

An inner thigh tattoo, also known as a ‘thigh piece’, is a tattoo placed on the inner thigh of a woman. The inner thigh is a popular spot for women to get tattoos as it is usually covered up, but can be easily revealed when desired. Inner thigh tattoos are usually done in a variety of styles, from tribal to floral, and range from small and simple to large and intricate. They can be placed anywhere from just above the knee to the top of the thigh, and can be as simple or as detailed as the woman desires.
