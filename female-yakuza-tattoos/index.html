The Symbolism Behind Female Yakuza Tattoos

Written by Feb 18, 2023 · 1 min read

Female Yakuza tattoos have become a popular trend in the tattoo industry over the past few decades. The Yakuza is a type of organized crime syndicate in Japan and the tattoos are typically associated with the gangsters of this group. These tattoos are usually brightly colored and often include kanji, dragons, and other iconic symbols. They are often inked on the arms, chest, and back, and are meant to symbolize strength, loyalty, and courage.
