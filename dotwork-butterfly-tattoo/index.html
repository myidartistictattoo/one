A Comprehensive Guide To Dotwork Butterfly Tattoos

Written by Feb 22, 2023 · 1 min read

Dotwork butterfly tattoos have become increasingly popular in recent years, with the unique style of tattooing offering an array of intricate and beautiful designs. The process of dotwork tattooing creates a unique look and feel, as the artist works with small dots to create the desired image. Dotwork tattoos are typically done in black ink, creating a bold, eye-catching look. They can also be done in color, but the black ink allows for a more detailed and intricate design.
