Tattoo butterflies are a type of tattoo that is typically made up of two or more butterflies that are drawn in a symmetrical pattern. The style of the tattoo is typically a combination of traditional tattooing techniques and a modern, creative twist. Tattoo butterflies can be drawn on any part of the body and can range from simple to complex designs. They are often accompanied by other elements, such as flowers, stars, or other animals.
