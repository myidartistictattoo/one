Butterflies are beautiful insects that come in all sorts of vibrant colors and patterns. They have become a popular choice for tattoos due to their beauty and symbolism. Butterflies are a symbol of transformation and growth, as they go through a metamorphosis from an egg to a caterpillar, and then to a beautiful winged creature. A butterfly tattoo can remind you to appreciate the changes in life and to find beauty in the process.
