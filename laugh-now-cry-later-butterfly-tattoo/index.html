Laugh Now Cry Later Butterfly Tattoo

Written by Mar 27, 2023 · 1 min read

Laugh Now Cry Later tattoo is a popular tattoo design that is inspired by the phrase ‘Laugh Now, Cry Later’. The phrase is associated with the idea that life is full of ups and downs, and it’s important to enjoy the good times while they last. The tattoo is usually designed with a laughing face and a crying face, along with a butterfly. The butterfly is intended to represent the transformation of life, and how it can change in an instant.
