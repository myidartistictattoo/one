A shoulder tattoo is an ink design that is done on the shoulder area and is one of the most popular locations for a tattoo. Shoulder tattoos are usually inked on the upper arm, but can also be found on the lower arm, or even the collarbone. These tattoos can be large or small, depending on the design and the area of the body that it is being inked on. Shoulder tattoos are a great way to express yourself and show off your creative side.
