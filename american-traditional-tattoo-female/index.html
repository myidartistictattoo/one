An American traditional tattoo is a style of tattooing that originated in the United States in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. It is characterized by bold lines and simple color palettes, often featuring patriotic red, white, and blue. The iconic designs are often recognizable for their bold outlines and vibrant colors, making them a popular choice for many people.
