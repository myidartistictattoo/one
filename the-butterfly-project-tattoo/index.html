The Butterfly Project Tattoo is a phenomenon that has been taking the world by storm in the past few years. The project encourages people to get a butterfly tattoo, or &quot;butterfly mark&quot; on their body, as a symbol of strength and a reminder to be kind to themselves and others. The Butterfly Project has become so popular that people from all over the world are getting it and sharing it with their friends and family.
