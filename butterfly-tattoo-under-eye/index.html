Butterfly Tattoo Under Eye – A Rising Trend In 2023

Written by Mar 25, 2023 · 1 min read

A butterfly tattoo under eye is a rising trend in the body art world. It is a delicate design of a butterfly drawn on the skin near the eyes. The butterfly tattoo under eye is usually drawn on the lower lid and is an attractive and interesting choice for people who want to express their individuality. The butterfly is often seen as a symbol of transformation, freedom, and beauty, and the tattoo can be a beautiful reminder of these qualities.
