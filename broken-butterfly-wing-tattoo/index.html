The Meaning Behind Broken Butterfly Wing Tattoos

Written by Mar 29, 2023 · 1 min read

Broken butterfly wing tattoos are a popular form of body art, often chosen by individuals who have suffered a traumatic event or are struggling with the idea of mortality. The butterfly is a powerful symbol of transformation, and the broken wings represent the fragility of life. When combined, these two elements create a powerful tattoo design that can help to symbolize the difficult journey that life can bring.
