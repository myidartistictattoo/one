Rib Tattoos For Women: A Guide For 2023

Written by Feb 14, 2023 · 1 min read

A rib tattoo can be an incredibly meaningful and beautiful way to express yourself. The rib cage is an especially popular spot for tattoos right now, with 2021 and 2022 seeing a huge surge of people getting inked along their sides. Rib tattoos can be incredibly striking, drawing attention to your waist, back, and chest. Plus, since it’s an area of the body that’s not constantly exposed, you’ll be able to show off your artwork when you want, and hide it when you don’t.
