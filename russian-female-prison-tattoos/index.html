Russian Female Prison Tattoos: What You Should Know

Written by Feb 19, 2023 · 1 min read

Russian female prison tattoos are body markings that have been used as a form of communication and identification among women in Russian prisons. The tattoos are often used to denote rank and status within the prison system, as well as to indicate a personal history or life story. The tattoos can range from simple symbols and images to intricate designs that cover the entire body.
