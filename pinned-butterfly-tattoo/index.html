Pinned Butterfly Tattoo: A Guide To What Makes This Trendy Design Stand Out

Written by Apr 07, 2023 · 1 min read

Butterfly tattoos have been a popular choice for body art for many years, with their elegant wings and vivid color combinations offering eye-catching visuals. Recently, however, a new style of butterfly tattoo has become a popular trend: the pinned butterfly tattoo. This trend is characterized by a butterfly that looks like it is pinned down by a sharp object, such as a needle or pin. If you’ve been considering getting a butterfly tattoo, this style is definitely worth exploring.
