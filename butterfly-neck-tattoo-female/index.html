Butterfly Neck Tattoo For Females: A Trend That Is Here To Stay

Written by Feb 23, 2023 · 1 min read

A butterfly neck tattoo is a popular choice for many women who are looking to make a statement with their body art. The tattoo consists of a single butterfly, usually depicted in black or gray ink, placed above the collar bone on the neck. While the butterfly is the most common design for this type of tattoo, there are other variations that include flowers, birds, stars, and other symbols. The tattoo can be as small or as large as the wearer desires. It is an elegant and beautiful way to express one’s personality and style.
