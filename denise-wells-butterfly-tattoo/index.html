Denise Wells Butterfly Tattoo: A Popular Trend In 2023

Written by Mar 12, 2023 · 1 min read

A Denise Wells butterfly tattoo is a design usually featuring a butterfly or moth with the name “Denise Wells” written in a script font. It is a popular trend in the tattoo world, especially amongst young adults. The tattoo is often seen as a tribute to a loved one who has passed away, and is often used as a way to remember them fondly. The tattoo also represents freedom, transformation and a new beginning. It is often placed on the wrist, shoulder or ankle, although it can be placed anywhere on the body.
